Roasted Cauliflower With Mashed Poatoes and Beetroots Ingredients:
    1 cauliflower, about 1 medium-large head
    Extra virgin olive oil, to coat
    Sea salt
    Black pepper
    10 small/medium sized potatoes
    ¼ cup butter
    ½ cup of Milk
    300 grams of marinated beetroots

Roasted Cauliflower With Mashed Poatoes and Beetroots Instructions:
    Cauliflower - Preheat oven to 200 degrees. Place cauliflower in a large mixing bowl. Pour on enough olive oil to coat, season generously with salt and pepper and toss gently until evenly coated.
    Lay cauliflower pieces out on a baking sheet. Drizzle any remaining oil from the bowl on top. Bake, turning once, until caramelized on edges and tender, 25 to 30 minutes.
    For mash potatoes - Boil the potatoes, after it’s boiled add salt, milk and butter and mash the everything together.
    For the beetroots - Just cut it in small pieces, drizzle some oil and it’s ready.
