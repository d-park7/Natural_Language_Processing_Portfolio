Ingredients
    • 1.5 oz White Claw Black Cherry Vodka
    • 0.25 oz Triple Sec or Cointreau
    • 0.75 oz Lemon Juice
    • 0.5 oz Ginger Syrup
    • Maraschino Cherry garnish  
Instructions
    1.  In an ice filled cocktail shaker pour White Claw Vodka, orange liqueur, lemon juice and ginger syrup.
    2.  Shake vigorously for 30-45 seconds until chilled.
    3.  Strain into an ice filled rocks glass.
    4.  Garnish with a maraschino cherry then serve immediately.
